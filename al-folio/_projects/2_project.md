SansadGPT is a cross-platform chatbot for natural-language querying over structured and unstructured parliamentary records.

**Stack:** Python, FAISS, LangChain, Hugging Face APIs, embeddings, document mining, NLP preprocessing, retrieval-augmented generation.

**Impact:** reduced manual search time across heavyweight PDFs by **85%** compared with manual question lookup workflows.
