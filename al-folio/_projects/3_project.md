ViBe is a benchmark for evaluating hallucinations in text-to-video diffusion models, developed with USC AIISC and accepted at ACL TrustNLP 2025.

**Research + engineering highlights**

- Built a 3,500+ video dataset for multimodal hallucination analysis.
- Fine-tuned and evaluated video understanding models such as VideoMAE and TimeSformer.
- Developed model comparison and mitigation workflows for safer multimodal generation.
