SeQuiFi studies continual learning for speech emotion recognition where models must absorb new emotion classes without erasing earlier knowledge.

**Tech focus:** speech foundation models, PyTorch training loops, continual learning, EWC/replay/model-soup baselines, evaluation pipelines, CKA-informed objectives.
